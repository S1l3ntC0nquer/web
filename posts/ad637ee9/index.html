Qingyun Road: About us, Mingdao Debate Club

Non-text

The following is what we wrote based on three years of experience when we first stepped down from our cadre positions. This is for reference for future students, and also hopes that More people can understand the true meaning of the debate.

Even now, when I re-read these words, I still feel deeply the impact those three years had on my life, so I copy them here. Although I don‚Äôt know how long this article or this spirit can exist in this form, I at least hope that I can safely guard this memory.

Preface: Debate?

Debate, in the eyes of most people, is just a group of people getting together to argue. Perhaps it is because of the established impression given by debates in Kathana‚Äôs political arena, or perhaps because of the feelings given by the word itself, that everyone has such a stereotype about debates. But real debate, as the art of persuasion, is more than just ‚Äúquarrels‚Äù. In fact, just using the word ‚Äúquarrel‚Äù to describe this activity would be too violent and flat. The world of debate is more exciting and more varied than ordinary people imagine. Every question is a journey of speculation; and in every competition, you can see the beauty of elegant sensibility and rationality intertwined.

As retired cadres of Mingdao Debate Club, we want to let you know what you can get in Mingdao Debate Club? And through this explanation, the outline of the debate competition is also sketched. If you want to get a glimpse of a more comprehensive debate, then come and join Mingdao‚Äôs Debate Club and participate in the competition!

Data collection, analysis and disassembly capabilities

First of all, before the competition, we need to collect a large amount of background data and data on both sides of the debate topic as evidence or supplementary arguments. For example, our semi-final topic in the 2023 National Kathana University Elite Cup is ‚ÄúCreators using artificial intelligence drawing has more benefits than disadvantages for the development of art/the disadvantages outweigh the benefits.‚Äù When preparing for this topic, we need to collect About Artificial Intelligence Drawing A large amount of data, including papers and reports. In addition, for issues such as ‚Äúthe definition of art‚Äù and ‚Äúthe value of art‚Äù, it is also necessary to conduct comprehensive data collection in order to clearly understand the requirements of the topic and formulate strategies. When collecting data, we will train our ability to ‚Äúfind data, read, and analyze‚Äù. We need to summarize and classify a piece of data to extract the key points of the text, so that it can be effectively used on the field. At the same time, because of the continuous and extensive collection of data, every time we finish a debate topic, we can have a certain degree of understanding of the field and become a small expert in that field.

Thinking from multiple perspectives and clear context

After collecting the data, we need to write a draft of the opening argument and guess the offense and defense that we may encounter on the court. When writing articles, we will use a rigorous structure to demonstrate the debate topic. Every part and every stage of the discussion requires data support. Through the stacking of a large amount of data and the connection of its own argument logic chain, a high-quality argument can be completed. At this stage, we can train a detailed thinking context. When writing an offensive and defensive table, you have to dismantle your own arguments from another standpoint, whether it is from data verification or logical disassembly. To put it simply, you have to have an imaginary game with yourself to predict what you may encounter. problem. By changing our own positions, we can think about a single issue from the perspective of ‚Äúpros and cons‚Äù, and we can also see the issue more comprehensively. Moreover, even within the same position, there may be different perspectives and different arguments. It‚Äôs like we look at the same issue differently from the government‚Äôs perspective, the people‚Äôs perspective, the business perspective, the micro perspective, the macro perspective, the short-term perspective, and the long-term perspective. By trying to think from different perspectives, we can inspire more novel and powerful arguments, and this also cultivates our diverse thinking perspectives, allowing us to think more three-dimensionally when thinking about issues.

The quality of empathy

Although there are only two perspectives in the competition, pro and con, in preparation, we will deeply discuss the values ‚Äã‚Äãwe defend, listen to the thoughts of different people from the heart, and reach out to various groups. Taking ‚ÄúPet Breeding Farms Should Be Banned in China‚Äù as an example, we found that most pet shops in Kathana ignore the animals‚Äô right to survival and keep them in captivity in inhumane ways; although cats, dogs, etc. may not have complex emotions, in our After seeing various negative reports about pet shops, I felt sad for the plight of pets. I also discovered the structural problems behind many appearances. At the same time, I paid more attention to animal rights and no longer dared to step into QQ, a pet shop. Or this year‚Äôs topic, ‚ÄúAdults in China should obtain consent from a physician before undergoing gender reassignment surgery.‚Äù When we defend the rights of transgender people, we must fully understand the legal, medical, and other restrictions placed on transgender people. various restrictions, and how to explain sexual/gender diversity in sociology, so that we can put ourselves in their shoes and play a beautiful game. Each issue and ethnic group has its own story. In this preparation process, we have strengthened our ability to perceive society, allowing us to better shape our understanding of this society and respect each different group. ‚ÄúCommon sense‚Äù makes debating not only a competition, but also a concern for society.

Practice opportunities for oral transmission

When debating, one of the important skills is oral expression. At the beginning, everyone may not speak very fluently, often stuttering, lagging, or using words that are too colloquial. However, in the debate field, it is very important to allow the other party and the referee to accept your words to the greatest extent possible. Discussion, at this time the ability of expression becomes very important. Whether it is us or many friends we know, we often use personal time such as taking a bath to practice speaking out our arguments fluently; or when we are responsible for reading a manuscript for a debate, we will practice reading the manuscript fluently again and again. In the past, some schools even read newspapers every day to train the clarity of pronunciation, the fluency of speech, and even the cadence of intonation. What‚Äôs interesting is that because each session is usually only four and a half minutes, many people have developed super fast speaking speeds, speaking almost two thousand words in two hundred and seventy seconds, and their aura alone is very powerful. In addition, practicing precise expression can also enable each person to use more precise vocabulary to describe what he wants to say when speaking or writing.

When you get on stage, the most important thing is not to be nervous. You may all have the experience of not being able to perform well when giving class reports in class because of nervousness; but after many debate competitions and practicing speaking freely in front of everyone, I believe that everyone can develop a stable typhoon that will allow you to No stage fright when expressing opinions in front of others. There are also many people who have developed their own fixed speaking style in this process, and it has become a personal characteristic!

Club atmosphere and senior sister resources

You may have heard about the strict senior sister system in the debate circle. In fact, what I said is true, because there is no role of ‚Äúteacher‚Äù in the debate club. Here is a group of senior sisters who came back spontaneously out of their love for the club and debate. The circle is supported by the inheritance of valuable experience, so whether it is due to experience or ability, everyone will maintain a respectful attitude towards their predecessors.

but! Don‚Äôt worry, because the seniors in the Mingdao Debate Club are more like our mentors and helpful friends. In normal conversations, we will use ‚Äúhome‚Äù to refer to this warm place.

Due to the special nature of the debating club, we focus our main efforts on participating in competitions, and the topics of the competition cover a wide range of fields, including pacifier entertainment, gender reassignment surgery, artificial intelligence, etc., covering a wide range of areas, but we are limited What we have learned is often not enough to support us in establishing a detailed imagination of the debate topic. Even if we have the omnipotent Google master in our hands, we often cannot find Data because the keywords entered are not suitable. At this time, the senior sister will appear like a god to rescue us, because the members of the debate club are very diverse, everyone has different interests, and they are also heading into different professional fields, including law, mathematics, salary, society, industry and commerce, etc. It‚Äôs also a big deal, so during the preparation process, we can often get the senior sister‚Äôs unique views on the debate topics based on her own major.

Of course, the excellent tradition of passing on experience also applies to things other than debate. Senior sisters often provide us with life or life advice. For example, how to balance schoolwork and activities in the first and second years of high school, what topics to choose for special research, whether to participate in youth summit forums, etc., as well as how to choose a university in the third year of high school, ingenuity in creating a learning process, and experience in preparing for interviews. etc.

All in all, as mentioned at the beginning, this will be your home. When you invest your enthusiasm and efforts into this club, there will be a group of people who also love this activity to help you grow, and at the same time give you support, encouragement, and constructive opinions, and a sense of belonging that extends beyond just three years of high school.

Hold national competitions

Every September, we will cooperate with Xiaoming Girls‚Äô High School, Taichung No. 1 Middle School, Taichung Senior High School, as well as Taichung Girls‚Äô High School and Wenhua High School, which joined last year. Each school will appoint one or two representatives to serve as directors to jointly organize the ‚ÄúRuide Cup‚Äù National High School Vocational Debate Competition".

There are two advantages to participating in event planning. First, each manager can learn the practical skills corresponding to the position. For example, I served as the CEO in the competition. During the preparation period, I could learn to coordinate and coordinate the opinions of each school, organize cadres to hold meetings, and supervise each school. The plan is completed on time; during the official competition period, you need to learn to deal with all unexpected things, such as various inexplicable complaints, etc. Welcome to join us, here your heart will be trained to be big, help You face difficulties calmly and appropriately and avoid being scolded.

Second, with the experience passed down by seniors, we can avoid many detours in the planning process, and if something goes wrong, seniors will accompany you to face it together. In this way, participating members can learn to take responsibility. At the same time, there is no need to bear huge pressure.

Conclusion: Beyond competition

Mingdao Debate has a history of only more than ten years. Compared with other schools with a long history, we are actually having a hard time catching up, and the gap in resources also makes it very difficult for us. But just like Books, debate is not just about the results, but more importantly the process of expending blood, and the emotions that transcend victory and defeat. If you have truly been involved in it, even if you go to college, leave the society, or enter the workplace, you will still cherish those days of working together, just like any team that dedicates its heart to working together.

Finally, I conclude with the words of Wen Langdong, a political commentator from Soochow University‚Äôs Zhengyan Club level 91:
